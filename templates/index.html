About Me

Quick facts:


- Currently a Grade 12 Challenge Student at Mount Doug Secondary in Victoria, BC.
- Completed four years of Drafting in school and been a Teacher Assistant twice
- Recently employed by Bradley Shuya Architect as a junior technician.
-Entering UVIC's Engineering program in the fall

See some of my recent work.

I'm passionate about design and it's potential to change the world.


I’ve taken four courses on Drafting and Design, and have been positively enthralled. I love imagining a design to make people’s lives better and bringing it to life through CAD. I’ve designed furniture, bike wheels, kitchens, and urban settings such as a parklet as part of my second year of Drafting. While I’m relatively new with four years of experience with Vectorworks in school, I’m a strong student who’s shown a lot a potential using it and I’m a fast learner who loves buildings and design.
